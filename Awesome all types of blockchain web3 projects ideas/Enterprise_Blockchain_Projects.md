# What is Enterprise Blockchain?

Enterprise Blockchain refers to blockchain systems designed for businesses, governments, and institutions where privacy, scalability, governance, compliance, and security are essential.

Unlike public blockchains, enterprise blockchains often use permissioned networks where participants are authenticated and governed by organizational policies.
