# What are Real World Assets (RWAs)?

**Real World Assets (RWAs)** are physical or legally recognized assets represented digitally on a blockchain through tokenization.

Tokenization enables fractional ownership, transparent transfers, programmable financial logic, and efficient settlement while remaining connected to the underlying legal asset.

Examples include:

* Real Estate
* Precious Metals
* Government Bonds
* Corporate Bonds
* Stocks
* Invoices
* Carbon Credits
* Art
* Vehicles
* Intellectual Property
