## Recommended Tech Stack

For building enterprise-grade private blockchain applications:

* **Blockchain Platform:** Hyperledger Fabric, R3 Corda, or Hyperledger Besu
* **Backend:** Node.js, Java, Go, or Spring Boot
* **Frontend:** React, Angular, or Vue.js
* **Database:** PostgreSQL, CouchDB (commonly used with Hyperledger Fabric), or MongoDB
* **Identity:** OAuth 2.0, LDAP, or enterprise PKI
* **Containerization:** Docker and Kubernetes
* **Messaging:** Apache Kafka or RabbitMQ
* **Cloud:** AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, or on-premises infrastructure

These projects emphasize controlled access, privacy, compliance, and auditability, making them well suited for enterprise and government environments where participants are known and trusted.
