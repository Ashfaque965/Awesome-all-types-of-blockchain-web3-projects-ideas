# What is a Layer-1 Blockchain?

A **Layer-1 (L1)** blockchain is the foundational blockchain network responsible for maintaining consensus, validating transactions, producing blocks, securing the network, and storing the canonical ledger.

Examples include:

* Bitcoin
* Ethereum
* Solana
* Avalanche
* Sui
* Aptos
* Cosmos Hub
* Polkadot Relay Chain
