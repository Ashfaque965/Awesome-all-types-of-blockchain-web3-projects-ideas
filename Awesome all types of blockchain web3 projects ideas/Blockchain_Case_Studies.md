Blockchain technology has evolved from cryptocurrency into a global platform powering decentralized finance, digital identity, supply chains, gaming, enterprise systems, and artificial intelligence.

Studying real-world blockchain projects provides valuable insights into:

* System architecture
* Consensus mechanisms

* Scalability
