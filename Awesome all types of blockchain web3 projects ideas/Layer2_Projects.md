# What is Layer-2?

A **Layer-2 (L2)** blockchain is a scaling solution that operates on top of a Layer-1 blockchain to increase throughput, reduce transaction costs, and improve user experience while relying on the Layer-1 network for security and settlement.

Examples include:

* Optimistic Rollups
* ZK Rollups
* State Channels
* Validiums
* Plasma
* Sidechains (architecture differs from canonical L2s but often studied alongside them)

---

# Goals of Layer-2

* Increase TPS (Transactions Per Second)
* Reduce Gas Fees
* Faster Transaction Confirmation
* Improve Scalability
* Preserve Layer-1 Security
* Enable Mass Adoption
