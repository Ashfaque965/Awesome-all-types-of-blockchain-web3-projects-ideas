# What is DePIN?

**DePIN (Decentralized Physical Infrastructure Networks)** uses blockchain and token incentives to coordinate and operate real-world infrastructure through decentralized communities instead of centralized providers.

Participants contribute physical resources—such as storage, compute, bandwidth, sensors, or energy—and are rewarded through blockchain-based incentive mechanisms.

* Decentralized Ownership
* Token Incentives
* Physical Infrastructure
* Permissionless Participation
