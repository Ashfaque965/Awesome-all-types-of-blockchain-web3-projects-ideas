# What is an NFT?

A **Non-Fungible Token (NFT)** is a blockchain-based token that represents ownership or authenticity of a unique digital or physical asset.

Unlike fungible tokens (such as cryptocurrencies), each NFT has a unique identifier and metadata.

## Characteristics

* Unique
* Transferable
* Verifiable
* Programmable
* Scarce
* Interoperable
* Traceable

# NFT Standards

## ERC721

One unique token per asset.

Examples:

* Art
* Certificates
* Memberships

---

## ERC1155

Supports multiple token types.

Examples:

* Games
* Collectibles
* Marketplace inventory

---

## ERC2981

Royalty standard.

---

## ERC4907

Rental NFTs.

---

## ERC6551

Token-Bound Accounts.

6. Build minting interface
7. Integrate wallet connectivity

9. Perform security review
10. Deploy to a public testnet
11. Launch the collection
12. Monitor marketplace activity
